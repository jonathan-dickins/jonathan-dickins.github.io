Visualising 54,000 diamonds

I used circle packing to visualise the famous diamonds dataset, plotting a circle for each diamond with it's colour representing its carat weight

A diamond's carat weight does not increase linearly with its physical size, and you can see this in the figure. There is a cluster of higher-carat diamonds at the center, but high-carat diamonds come in all sizes
